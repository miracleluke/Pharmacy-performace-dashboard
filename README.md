#  Pharmacy Performance Dashboard

##  Project Overview

The Pharmacy Performance Dashboard is an interactive business intelligence solution developed in Tableau to analyze and monitor pharmacy operations across countries, product categories, pharmacy types, and product brands.

##  Business Problem

Pharmacy businesses generate large volumes of sales and operational data daily. However, without proper analysis, it becomes difficult to:
- Monitor overall business performance.
- Identify top-performing pharmacies.
- Understand revenue and profit drivers.
- Evaluate product profitability.
- Compare performance across and countries.
- Understand how promotions impact revenue?

This dashboard was designed to transform raw pharmacy data into meaningful business insights that support strategic decision-making.

##  Project Objectives

The primary objectives of this dashboard is to:
- Monitor overall pharmacy business performance
- Identify key revenue and profit drivers
- Analyze sales trends across countries and pharmacy types
- Compare year-over-year business growth
- Identify top-performing pharmacies, products, and brands
- Support management decision-making through interactive visualizations.

## Tools Used

- Tableau - Dashboard Development & Data Visualization
- Microsoft Excel - Data Preparation 
- PowerPoint - Presentation & Business Storytelling 
- GitHub - Project Documentation & Portfolio Hosting

##  Dashboard Features

- Dynamic Metric Selector - Users can switch between revenue, profit, units sold. This enables flexible performance analysis from multiple business perspectives.

- Country Filter - Allows users to compare business performance across different countries.

- Year-over-Year Analysis - Measures current-year performance against previous-year results to evaluate business growth.

- Dynamic Top 10 Rankings - Automatically updates rankings based on selected metrics and filters.

- Interactive Visualizations - KPI cards, bar charts, donut charts, geographic maps, trend visualizations.


## Dashboard Structure

The dashboard contains two interactive pages designed to provide both high-level performance monitoring and detailed driver analysis.

## Overview Dashboard

The overview dashboard provides a high-level summary of business performance and year-over-year comparisons across revenue, profit and units sold.

#### Key Insights Included

- Year-over-Year Growth Analysis
- Metrics by Season
- Metrics by Pharmacy Type
- Metrics by Country
- Metrics by Product Category

#### Business Questions Answered

- Is the business growing compared to the previous year?
- Which countries generate the most revenue compared to the previous year?
- Which pharmacy type performs best compared to the previous year?
- Are there seasonal patterns?

## Drivers Dashboard

The driver dashboard focuses on identifying the key factors driving business performance.

#### Key Insights Included

- Metrics by Promotion Flag
- Metrics by Pharmacy Size
- Metrics by Pharmacy Duration
- Metrics by Product Category
- Top 10 Product Brands
- Top 10 Pharmacies
- Top 10 Products

#### Business Questions Answered

- Do promotions significantly impact revenue?
- Which pharmacy sizes generate the most revenue?
- Which brands and products dominate revenue?
- Which pharmacies are top performers?
- What operational factors influence performance?


##  Key Insights Discovered

#### - Prescription Products Drive Revenue
Prescription products contribute the largest share of total revenue, highlighting their importance to overall business performance.

#### - Urban Pharmacies Perform Best
Urban pharmacy locations consistently outperform suburban and rural locations across key performance metrics.

#### - Promotions Have Limited Revenue Contribution
A significant portion of revenue is generated without promotional campaigns, suggesting opportunities to improve promotional effectiveness.

#### - Few Brands Dominate Sales
Top-performing brands such as antiBioX, glucoSafe and neuroMed contribute significantly to overall revenue.

#### - Large & Medium Pharmacies Outperform Small Pharmacies
Store size has a noticeable impact on revenue generation and overall business performance.


##  Business Recommendations

### 1. Increase Investment in Prescription Products

- Prioritize shelf placement and promotion of top-performing brands
- Negotiate bulk purchase discounts with suppliers
- Use these brands in marketing campaigns

### 2. Optimize Promotional Strategy

- Measure promotional return on investment (ROI)
- Test targeted promotions on slow-moving products
- Use discount campaigns strategically instead of broadly
- Analyze which promotions increase both volume and profit

### 3. Expand High-Performing Pharmacy Types

- Open more branches in urban areas
- Replicate successful urban strategies in suburban stores
- Optimize product mix based on location type. 

### 4. Prioritize High-Performing Brands

- Increase visibility of top-performing brands
- Negotiate supplier discounts
- Improve stock availability

### 5. Improve Performance of Smaller Pharmacies

- Analyze operational inefficiencies
- Review staffing, inventory, and customer traffic in small locations
- Introduce localized marketing campaigns
- Consider merging or restructuring underperforming stores

### 6. Leverage Seasonal Demand Patterns

- Prepare inventory ahead of high-demand seasons
- Run targeted seasonal campaigns
- Align staffing levels with expected demand periods

### 8. Prioritize High Margin & High Volume Products

- Increase visibility for high-margin products
- Bundle low-margin/high-volume items with premium products
- Reduce inventory for weak-performing products


## Conclusion

This dashboard reveals that revenue growth is primarily driven by prescription products, high-performing urban pharmacies and a few dominant product brands. By optimizing promotions, improving smaller pharmacy performance and expanding successful operational strategies, the business can improve in both revenue growth and profitability.
